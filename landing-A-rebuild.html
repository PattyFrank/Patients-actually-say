Questions before you download

1. Is this real research, or is it just thought-leadership content with a sales pitch attached?
Real research. The dataset is 7,823 mentor–patient conversations on the PatientPartner platform (Jan 2021–Mar 2026), cross-referenced with 12,400+ public forum posts, 14 peer-reviewed studies, and 3 national surveys. Methodology is documented in the report's final section — defensible to your medical, legal, and regulatory teams. There's no product pitch in the body of the report.
2. Will I get a sales call after I download this?
No, unless you ask. We'll send you the report and one short follow-up email two weeks later asking what was useful. That's it. If you want to talk to our team, there's a "Book a demo" link in the report — the choice is yours.
3. Can I share this internally with my team?
Yes. Forward it freely. The report is built to be circulated inside pharma marketing, PSP ops, medical affairs, and patient advocacy teams — that's the point. We just ask that citations remain intact when stats are quoted externally.
4. Which therapy areas does the data cover?
Six core areas with statistically meaningful sample sizes: autoimmune / biologics (n=2,147), surgical procedures (1,893), women's health (1,412), type 2 diabetes (1,204), oncology support (689), and medical devices (478). Findings are broken down by therapy area where the patterns differ.
5. How is this different from typical adherence or PSP benchmark reports?
Most adherence reports analyze claims data or survey responses. This one analyzes conversations — what patients actually say when they're talking to someone who's been there, not filling out a form for a vendor. That's what reveals the gap between clinical comprehension and behavioral commitment.
6. Is the data HIPAA-compliant and safe to reference internally?
Yes. All conversation data is de-identified per HIPAA Safe Harbor with IRB exemption on file. PatientPartner is HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2 certified, and built for regulated pharma / med-device environments. Your compliance team can review our methodology in the report appendix.
7. Can my team apply these findings without working with PatientPartner?
Absolutely. The 5 implications in the report are structural and platform-agnostic — you can apply them inside your existing PSP, your existing vendor stack, or your in-house team. We'd love to be a partner if our peer-mentorship approach fits, but the report stands on its own.
